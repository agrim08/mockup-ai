# Design Guidelines for UI/UX Mockup AI

## Color & Gradient Usage
- **Use light, subtle gradients** - avoid heavy, overpowering gradient effects
- Prefer single colors with subtle gradient accents
- Gradients should enhance, not dominate the design

## Interaction Design
- **Add micro-interactions** to all interactive elements
- Focus on subtle, delightful animations
- Smooth transitions and hover effects
- Scale, translate, and opacity changes for feedback

## General Principles
- Clean, modern aesthetic
- Premium feel without being overwhelming
- Responsive and accessible design
- Consistent with indigo/violet color scheme
